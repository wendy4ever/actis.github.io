About Us

Auckland Chinese Translation & Interpreting Service Ltd (ACTIS) is a translation agency based in Auckland, New Zealand. With the employing a certified translator and interpreter, we provide translation services focusing on English to Chinese and Chinese to English, and we also offer various translation services for overseas students, visa applications, local communities as well as government departments. Our translation and interpreting services cover multiple industries, such as business, legal, technical, medical ones. We aim to guarantee our professional translator and interpreter immerses in the environment to maintain their language skills, to keep the accuracy of their expressions in all aspects of the linguistic field and to provide a highly processional translation and interpreting services tailored to the New Zealand market.
